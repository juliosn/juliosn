## About Me

👋 Hello! I am a back-end developer, passionate about challenges and technological innovation. Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Systems Analysis and Development at Fatec Zona Leste, aiming to enhance my skills and expand my knowledge in this fascinating and vast world of computing.

💼 I currently work as a technology consultant, focusing on back-end development and data science. I enjoy taking on new challenges and learning while working, which keeps me curious and motivated. I have a particular interest in data science and software engineering, areas I intend to explore more deeply. My approach is simple: use what I know to make a difference in projects and, along the way, absorb as much knowledge as possible!

🖥️ My skills include:
- Back-end development using Java, Python, C++, and other languages.
- Use of modeling languages and project documentation tools, such as UML.
- Proficiency in English for technical and professional communication.
